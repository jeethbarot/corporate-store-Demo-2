About BrandForge

We've been the go-to corporate merchandise partner for Canadian businesses since 2011. Proudly Canadian, coast to coast.

Our Mission

BrandForge exists to take the friction out of corporate merchandise. We know that sourcing, customizing, and delivering branded products for hundreds or thousands of employees is genuinely hard — and we've built our entire operation around making it easy, predictable, and scalable.

From onboarding kits for new hires to branded giveaways at industry events, we deliver quality merchandise on deadline. Our clients range from growing startups to established Canadian enterprises, and we treat every order — large or small — with the same care and attention.

Our Process

When you partner with BrandForge, you're assigned a dedicated account manager who becomes an extension of your team. They'll help you pick the right products, configure custom branding, manage approvals, and coordinate delivery — so your team focuses on what matters.

Every product is quality-checked before it leaves our warehouse. We work exclusively with ISO-certified manufacturers and conduct multi-point inspections on all outbound orders. Our 98% on-time delivery rate speaks for itself.

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible sourcing. Our product catalogue includes a growing range of sustainably produced items, recycled materials, and ethically certified factories. Ask your account manager about our eco-range.
